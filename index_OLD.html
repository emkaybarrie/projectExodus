About

Project Exodus is building a new kind of connected experience — where finance feels like play, progress is visible, and every step opens doors. For players, it’s a world that rewards better habits. For institutions and partners, it’s a platform to engage audiences in fresh, meaningful ways. Together, we’re creating an ecosystem where utility and imagination move hand in hand.

Our Approach

We merge playful design with practical utility. Systems that feel good to use, reward good habits, and never compromise your data.

Partnerships

We believe progress is shared. From financial institutions and technology providers to creators and educators, we collaborate with partners who bring unique strengths. Together we design connected experiences that empower players and expand what’s possible.

Ecosystem

Our platform is built to integrate — from banking APIs and digital wallets to lifestyle services and brand activations. Every connection enriches the world, giving players meaningful ways to act on their progress while amplifying partner reach.

What’s Next

The journey ahead is collaborative. We’re opening with the MyFi HUD and real-world finance integrations, then layering quests, credits, avatars, and partner perks. Each milestone creates new opportunities to co-create value for players and partners alike.
