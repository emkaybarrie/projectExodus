Why MyFi exists

Most money tools talk in graphs and guilt. MyFi speaks in stories, rhythms and small wins – a calm space to practise, slip up and try again without being judged.

Games can teach healthier rhythms

We borrow the good bits from games – vitals, quests and avatars – so pacing your spending feels more like managing a character than fighting your bank balance.

About MyFi

The world where your money finally makes sense.

Health Mana Stamina Essence Emberward

Behind the scenes, MyFi evens out the spikes and dips in your month and turns them into a steady rhythm – simple energy bars that show how fast you’re burning through cash and how quickly you’re recovering. You don’t have to guess if you’re “doing okay” this month – your bars show whether you’re shielded, coasting or strong enough to take on more.

  • Behaviour engine – tidies up the mess of real-life spending so your month feels smoother, not like a series of mini heart attacks.
  • Vitals HUD – turns your finances into game-style energy bars – your “health”, “power” and “stamina” – that you can read in seconds instead of decoding a spreadsheet.
  • Quests & Avatars – a story-driven game layer to explore with your avatar, where you can reap the rewards of your improved spending and saving habits.
  • The Badlands – build and protect the city of Myana, delve deeper into The Badlands with your avatar, and reap the rewards of your improved spending and saving habits.

Partnerships

From local pilots to nationwide rollouts. MyFi is built to sit between young people and the systems around them – community support, public services, universities and banks – turning money stress into a calmer, more playable journey for everyone involved.

Advice & community organisations

A gentle, non-judgemental space where people can see their month as energy and breathing room, instead of pure debt and panic. Frontline teams stay focused on the hard conversations; MyFi handles the everyday check-ins in a way that feels visual, hopeful and easy to come back to.

Public sector & education

A prevention-first tool that fits into cost-of-living, resilience and youth wellbeing programmes. MyFi helps young people notice problems earlier, build small buffers and stay engaged between appointments, so support isn’t just a one-off moment.

Banks & financial services

A behaviour-led wellbeing layer that sits on top of existing accounts. MyFi helps customers pace their spending, protect the essentials and actually enjoy opening your app – supporting duty, inclusion and long-term loyalty without pushing extra debt.

Why we’re building MyFi

MyFi started with a simple pattern: smart, capable people saying “I know I should look at my money, but I just can’t face it.” We want to build a space where money feels understandable, where mistakes are part of the story not the end of it, and where banks and public bodies can genuinely help young people stay out of harm’s way while still letting them play, experiment and grow.
