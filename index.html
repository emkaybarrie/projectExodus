About MyFi

The behaviour-first engine behind the world of MyFi.

Health Mana Stamina Essence Emberward

MyFi normalises your income and spending into smooth regen curves, then translates those into clear energy bars. Instead of guessing if you’re “okay”, you see whether your vitals are protected, stable or growing.

  • Behaviour engine – classifies, normalises and smooths cashflow.
  • Vitals HUD – shows Health, Mana, Stamina, Essence and Emberward.
  • Quests & world – optional game layer that rewards good pacing.

Why we’re building MyFi

MyFi grew out of years of watching people feel crushed by complex financial systems. The goal is to create a space where money feels understandable, where mistakes don’t define you, and where banks and public bodies can genuinely help people stay out of harm’s way.
