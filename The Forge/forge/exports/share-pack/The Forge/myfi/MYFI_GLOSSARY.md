# MyFi Glossary

## Vitals & currency
- **Health:** emergency buffer / survival energy
- **Mana:** intentional / power spending pool
- **Stamina:** day-to-day flexible spending pool
- **Essence:** long-term savings/investment potential; accrues through surplus and is ethically constrained
- **Emberward / Shield:** protective budgeting construct for essentials (conceptual/partial)

## Architecture
- **Surface:** screen/layout description (config-first)
- **Slot:** named mount region within a surface
- **Part:** reusable UI unit mounted into a slot; may have uplift layers
- **Journey:** thin orchestration sequence over screens/modals/features
- **Feature Pack:** stable capability provider (data, actions) consumed by UI

## Modes
- **Verified:** tied to real financial signals and stricter safeguards
- **Unverified:** manual/demonstration mode with limits and guardrails
