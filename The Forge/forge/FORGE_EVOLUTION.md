1. Principle of Directed Self-Evolution

The Forge is explicitly aware that it is building itself toward automation.

Therefore:

Every approved process change must:

reduce ambiguity

reduce repeated explanation

increase artifact-driven execution

or unlock future automation hooks

Process changes that do not advance this trajectory are invalid.

2. What May Evolve

The following may be evolved through this protocol:

Forge process rules

Artifact structures

Required files and their locations

Agent prompting and enforcement behaviour

Review cadence and feedback loops

Automation readiness criteria

The following may not evolve implicitly:

Forge Kernel invariants

Authority hierarchy

Dual-track doctrine

Narrative-first Director accommodation

Those require explicit Director approval and Kernel update.
