# MyFi Onboarding Validation Checklist (Director)

Purpose: Allow the Director to confirm “this represents everything we know and accept right now”.

## Validation gates

### A) Identity & intent
- [ ] The MyFi purpose statement matches current vision
- [ ] The Vitals definitions are correct (Health/Mana/Stamina/Essence)
- [ ] Ethical framing (Essence vs premium) matches your intent

### B) Architectural truth (high-level)
- [ ] Mobile-first, no-build ESM is accurate
- [ ] Surfaces/Slots/Parts model is the chosen direction
- [ ] Hub/Vitals is the canonical anchor
- [ ] Quests is the reference screen for workflow stabilisation

### C) What is stable vs in flux
- [ ] Stable list is accurate
- [ ] In-flux list is accurate
- [ ] Risks are accurate

### D) Status matrix sanity
- [ ] Statuses reflect reality
- [ ] Notes reflect current constraints

### E) Capsule quality
- [ ] Capsule is short enough to share
- [ ] Capsule is specific enough to prevent drift

## Seal decision
- [ ] Approved as current truth
- [ ] Approved with edits (update files before sealing)
- [ ] Not approved (list required changes)

Once approved, create/update:
- MYFI_SNAPSHOT_SEAL.md
